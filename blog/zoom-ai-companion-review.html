Features of Zoom AI Companion (That I Actually Like!)

Meeting Questions with AI Companion

Suppose an attendee comes in late for a meeting, and let’s face it, we have all been there. We are curious if we missed something or whether our name was called while we were away. We would like to get up to speed but hesitate to ask because it will disrupt the flow of the meeting. The AI Companion can help someone who joined a Zoom meeting a few minutes late by summarizing what has been discussed so far.

Image Source

With Zoom AI Companion, meeting participants have the capability to get themselves up to speed by asking questions in the Zoom AI Companion chat during the meeting without disrupting the meeting.

‍

Whiteboard Feature

When I first saw this in action, I was pretty amused. Post-meeting users can open the whiteboard feature and generate ideas by adding any prompt that is related to the meeting they just had and seeing a visual representation of it. There are many types of visual meeting content that you can choose from.

You can generate your ideas or queries in the form of:

  • Stickers
  • Tables
  • Mindmap
  • Flowchart

Suppose you just finished a meeting about a new project. Open the whiteboard feature to organize your ideas. You can use the Mindmap option to draw out a visual representation of the brainstorming topics, stickers to highlight key points, and tables to assign tasks. The possibilities are actually endless!

Pros and Cons Of Zoom AI Companion Features

✅ Innovative feature of the whiteboard. It especially helps with meetings that need a visual structure.

✅ Semantic analyses to know how the team members are feeling can add a nice personal touch to knowing how they feel. However, I’d rather not get analyzed like that; I would rather attend a meeting without having my feelings checked. A little bit of privacy, please. But I do understand the reason behind it, but still, I’d rather not have it checked on me.

✅ AI Companion can write responses, summarize chats, and prompt sharing of next steps in Zoom Team Chat.

✅ AI Companion is in the Zoom Team Chat window so you can connect with your team.

❌ You need to pay $14 monthly to use Zoom AI Companion without having a free plan or a free trial. In my opinion, users must be given the opportunity to try any software before they spend their hard-earned money.

❌ Speaker identification is limited to Zoom users, meaning if someone outside of the meeting spoke, for example, a team member next to you, it won’t be transcribed.

❌ No custom vocabulary. Users attend meetings that have different vocabularies depending on the industry. There would be difficult words or jargon spoken during the meeting that an AI might mishear and write words that are way off from what was originally said.

❌ Doesn’t transcribe in-person meetings, meaning if you were to run into a client or meet up with the client in person, you wouldn’t be able to use Zoom AI companion features because it unfortunately doesn’t support in-person meetings. So if you are in a field where you are meeting your prospects in person, a Zoom AI companion will not be the best choice for you.

❌ Speaker identification is limited to Zoom users, meaning if someone outside of the meeting spoke, for example, a team member next to you, it won’t be transcribed.

❌ It only works with the Zoom platform. So here’s the thing: users who have clients using multiple platforms won’t be able to use Zoom AI Companion to transcribe their meeting summary. This sets the user at a disadvantage and could potentially cause them to miss meetings because of compatibility issues.

What is Zoom's AI Companion?

Zoom's AI Companion, formerly known as Zoom IQ, is an AI-powered assistant integrated into the Zoom platform. It helps improve productivity by summarizing meetings, drafting emails, composing chat messages, and providing real-time feedback during meetings. It's available at no extra cost for users with paid Zoom accounts​

What AI Tools Does Zoom AI Companion Offer?

Zoom AI Companion offers various AI tools, including meeting summaries, email and chat message composition, real-time feedback during meetings, and smart recordings that divide cloud recordings into chapters for easy review​

How Does Zoom AI Companion Provide Real-Time Feedback?

During meetings, AI Companion can give real-time feedback on your communication, such as how often you're speaking versus listening. This helps you adjust your communication style in the moment to improve engagement and effectiveness

How Does Zoom AI Companion Help if I Miss a Meeting?

If you miss a meeting, AI Companion can generate a detailed summary highlighting key points, action items, and important discussions. This allows you to catch up quickly without needing to watch the entire meeting recording​
