Meet Jamie

Ready to build the future. Safe. Proactive. Magical.

How Jamie started

Jamie started out of our frustration of wasting time on busy-work. In our days, we spend countless hours on things that don't require us to actively think. Time that should be used for more useful activities. 

In early 2023 we launched jamie as an AI-based meeting note taking application that generates human-like notes for any meeting. Since then thousands of people have signed up for jamie and became trusted customers.

What we believe in

At our company, we share one common belief: AI will enable humans to do more with less.

Inspired by this belief, we are working hard on crafting a leading product that stands out through quality of craft, usefulness, and reliability. All to advance our mission of saving millions of hours a year for talented knowledge workers like you.

Our way ahead

Automated meeting notes are a great start, but not the finish line.

We are building out the product, and company, to solve problems people like you face before, during, and after meetings. After talking to thousands of users, we learned that there are a ton of mundane tasks we can eliminate from your life so you have more time for the things that matter to you.

From this, we are continuing to integrate vertically into the existing workflows of our users, all rooted in the existing behaviour of meetings.
